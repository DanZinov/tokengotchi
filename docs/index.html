How it works

You code. Your hero climbs.

No new habits, no extra effort. The signal is already on your disk — Tokengotchi just turns it into progress.

1

You code

Your AI agent writes token counts to local log files as you work — the same data ccusage reads. Nothing leaves your machine.

2

Tokens → energy

Tokengotchi converts usage into combat energy, streak-weighted so showing up beats raw spend. Your work is the fuel.

3

Come back to loot

Floors cleared, levels gained, gear dropped, bosses down — all earned while you were heads-down in the editor.

What's inside

A real idle game, not a gimmick.

Tuned combat math, juicy feedback, and a deep long-game: your hero visibly transforms, unlocks new attack styles, and climbs prestige ranks through themed zones.

⚔️

Idle auto-battler

Hit flashes, crits, knockback, damage numbers, particles and screen shake — plus new attack styles as you level: melee, ranged, multi-shot, then a screen-shaking beam.

🧙

Classes & builds

Vibe Coder (crit/glass), Refactorer (tank/sustain), Architect (balanced). Switch anytime.

💎

Loot & evolution

Common → Legendary drops with rarity glow, a loadout you can see, and a hero that visibly transforms — earning a weapon, aura, wings, a crown and orbiting orbs as it grows.

👑

Prestige & ranks

Reset past the first boss for a permanent, compounding multiplier and climb the ranks — Bronze, Silver, Gold… all the way to Mythic. Themed zones keep the depths fresh.

🔌

Reads your agents

Claude Code, Codex & Gemini out of the box (Cursor experimental). Add another with one small adapter.

🔒

Local & free

No account, no backend, no telemetry. The whole game is free — tips are a thank-you, never power.

Your work powers your hero — never your wallet.

Tokengotchi is built so burning more tokens isn't the goal: rewards are streak-weighted, it's PvE only, and money buys nothing but goodwill. It celebrates the coding you were already doing.
